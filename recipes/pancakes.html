Pancakes

Description

Looking for a fluffy pancake recipe? This one is just right! Topped with syrup or strawberries and whipped cream, they are impossible to resist.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Sugar
  • Milk and butter
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Sift the dry ingredients together.
  2. Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.
  3. Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan.
  4. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip.
  5. Continue cooking until brown on both sides.
